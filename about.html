ABOUT US

Discover the story behind our passion for aviation

Pioneering Aviation Excellence Since 2005

Founded with a vision to make aviation accessible to all, Aero Club has been at the forefront of aviation education and experiences for over 15 years. Our journey began with a small group of aviation enthusiasts and has grown into a community of thousands.

We believe that the sky is not the limit, but just the beginning. Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower individuals to explore the wonders of flight through hands-on experiences, expert guidance, and state-of-the-art facilities.

From flight simulations to actual flying experiences, we provide our members with opportunities to learn, grow, and connect with like-minded individuals who share a passion for aviation.

Our Mission

To democratize aviation education and make flying accessible to everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

Our Vision

To create a world where aviation inspires innovation, connects communities, and pushes the boundaries of human potential.

Our Values

Safety, innovation, inclusivity, and excellence guide everything we do at Aero Club.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones that shaped our aviation community

2005

Foundation

Aero Club was founded by a group of aviation enthusiasts with a vision to make flying accessible to all.

2008

First Flight Simulator

We acquired our first professional flight simulator, allowing members to experience flying in a safe environment.

2012

Partnership with Airlines

Established partnerships with major airlines for internship and training opportunities for our members.

2016

Aviation Scholarship Program

Launched our scholarship program to support aspiring pilots from underprivileged backgrounds.

2020

Digital Transformation

Expanded our reach through virtual events and online courses during the pandemic.

2023

New Training Facility

Opened a state-of-the-art training facility with advanced simulators and classrooms.
